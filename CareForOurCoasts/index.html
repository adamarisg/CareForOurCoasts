Care For Our Coasts

Let's learn to care for our oceans like they care for us!

For years plastics and other waste have compiled on beaches. Before plastic, these beaches were places where locals could fish and relax but because of a surplus in waste this is no longer an option. The waste that people leave on shores eventually leads itself into oceans. The trash affects both the marine life in the water and the life on land.
